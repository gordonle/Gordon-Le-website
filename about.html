About Me

I am..

a brother, a son, a Scorpio, and probably some other things too. I'm from the beautiful city of Ottawa, Ontario, where I was born and raised. I completed the International Baccalaureate Program at Colonel By Secondary School, and now I'm attending the University of Waterloo, studying Computer Science.

I am trilingual, and can speak English, French and Cantonese. I am also passionate about many things, as you'll soon find out below.

I have..

a pet turtle; her name is Walle. When I first got her, she was around the size of a tooney - now shes grown to the size of my hand.

I love..

my family, my friends, and all those that have given me guidance or help so that I can be where I am today. From the bottom of my heart, thank you.

I also love to volunteer. GACSN is a program for children with special needs, and volunteering there has taught me friendship, patience, love, and most of all has brought a unique sense of fulfillment to my life.

Along with that, I also love music. I have been playing music ever since I was very young, and my passion for it has only grown. In the 10th grade I started a small band with two of my friends, Curtis Lei and Jessica Liu. My first term at UW was also my first term as a member of The Water Boys, an all-male A Cappella group. On the side, I continue to create my own music, posting covers of some of my favourite songs here!

Lastly, I love to play badminton, swim, play ultimate frisbee, and workout. In high school, I played badminton competitively, and I was the captain of both the Ultimate Frisbee and Swim teams. My love for swimming transferred into pursuing a part-time job as a Swim Instructor and Lifeguard.
